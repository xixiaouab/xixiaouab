## About Me

I build efficient and reliable AI systems across language models, vision-language models, and computer vision. My recent work focuses on MLLM agents, reasoning, efficient adaptation, and foundation models for real-world scientific and medical applications.

- Core contributor to **ORBIT-2**, an exascale climate foundation model pretrained on Frontier.
- **SC 2025 Best Paper Award** and **ACM Gordon Bell Prize finalist** for ORBIT-2.
- Open to research collaborations on agents, post-training, efficient adaptation, and multimodal foundation models.
